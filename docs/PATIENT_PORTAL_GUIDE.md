## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

#### First-Time Registration

1. **Visit the Portal**: Go to [patient.pharmacycopilot.com](https://patient.pharmacycopilot.com)
2. **Click "Sign Up"**: Located in the top right corner
3. **Enter Your Information**:
   - Full name (as it appears on your prescriptions)
   - Date of birth
   - Phone number
   - Email address
   - Create a secure password

4. **Verify Your Identity**: 
   - Enter your pharmacy's verification code (provided by your pharmacist)
   - Or provide your prescription number from a recent medication

5. **Confirm Your Email**: Check your email and click the verification link

#### Account Security

- **Strong Password**: Use at least 8 characters with numbers and symbols
- **Two-Factor Authentication**: Enable for extra security (recommended)
- **Privacy**: Never share your login credentials with others

### Logging In

1. Go to [patient.pharmacycopilot.com](https://patient.pharmacycopilot.com)
2. Click "Sign In"
3. Enter your email and password
4. Click "Sign In"

**Forgot Your Password?**
1. Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for reset instructions
4. Follow the link to create a new password

### Step-by-Step Booking Process

#### Step 1: Choose Appointment Type

1. **Log into your portal**
2. **Click "Book Appointment"** on your dashboard
3. **Select appointment type** from the available options
4. **Read the description** to ensure it matches your needs

#### Step 2: Select Date and Time

1. **Choose your preferred date** using the calendar
   - Green dates have availability
   - Gray dates are unavailable
   - Today's date is highlighted in blue

2. **Select a time slot**
   - Available times are shown in green
   - Unavailable times are grayed out
   - Times show the pharmacist who will see you

3. **Consider your schedule**
   - Allow extra time for travel and parking
   - Consider your work schedule
   - Think about medication timing if relevant

#### Step 3: Provide Additional Information

1. **Reason for Visit** (optional but helpful)
   - Brief description of your concern
   - Specific questions you want to ask
   - Any symptoms you're experiencing

2. **Special Requirements** (if any)
   - Wheelchair accessibility needed
   - Language interpreter required
   - Preferred pharmacist (if available)

3. **Insurance Information**
   - Select your insurance plan
   - Provide member ID if required
   - Note any changes since last visit

#### Step 4: Review and Confirm

1. **Double-check all details**:
   - Date and time
   - Appointment type
   - Your contact information
   - Special requirements

2. **Choose reminder preferences**:
   - Email reminders (recommended)
   - Text message reminders
   - Phone call reminders
   - How far in advance you want reminders

3. **Click "Book Appointment"**

#### Step 5: Confirmation

1. **Confirmation screen** appears with:
   - Appointment confirmation number
   - Date, time, and location details
   - Pharmacist name
   - Preparation instructions

2. **Confirmation email** sent to your email address
3. **Calendar invite** attached (add to your personal calendar)
4. **Confirmation text** sent if you chose SMS notifications

### What to Bring to Your Appointment

#### For All Appointments
- **Photo ID** (driver's license, passport, etc.)
- **Insurance card** (if applicable)
- **List of current medications** (or bring the bottles)
- **List of allergies** and adverse reactions
- **Questions or concerns** written down

#### For Specific Appointment Types

**MTM Sessions:**
- All prescription medications
- Over-the-counter medications and supplements
- Recent lab results
- List of healthcare providers

**Health Screenings:**
- Previous test results for comparison
- List of family health history
- Current symptoms or concerns

**New Medication Consultations:**
- New prescription
- List of current medications
- Insurance information

**Vaccinations:**
- Vaccination record/card
- List of previous reactions to vaccines
- Travel itinerary (for travel vaccines)

## Managing Your Appointments

### Viewing Your Appointments

#### Dashboard Overview

When you log in, your dashboard shows:
- **Next Appointment**: Date, time, and type
- **Upcoming Appointments**: Next 30 days
- **Past Appointments**: History and records
- **Quick Actions**: Reschedule, cancel, or book new

#### Appointment Details

Click on any appointment to see:
- **Full Details**: Date, time, type, pharmacist
- **Preparation Instructions**: What to bring and do
- **Location Information**: Address, parking, directions
- **Contact Information**: Phone number for questions

### Rescheduling Appointments

#### When You Can Reschedule
- **Up to 24 hours before** your appointment
- **Emergency situations** (call the pharmacy directly)
- **Illness** that might affect others (reschedule online or call)

#### How to Reschedule

1. **Go to "My Appointments"**
2. **Find the appointment** you want to change
3. **Click "Reschedule"**
4. **Choose a new date and time** from available options
5. **Confirm the change**
6. **Receive new confirmation** via email/text

#### Rescheduling Limitations
- **Same-day changes**: Must call pharmacy directly
- **Multiple reschedules**: May require phone confirmation
- **Popular times**: May have limited availability

### Cancelling Appointments

#### How to Cancel

1. **Go to "My Appointments"**
2. **Find the appointment** to cancel
3. **Click "Cancel Appointment"**
4. **Select cancellation reason**:
   - No longer needed
   - Scheduling conflict
   - Illness
   - Other (please specify)
5. **Confirm cancellation**

#### Cancellation Policy
- **24+ hours notice**: No penalty, full refund if applicable
- **Less than 24 hours**: May incur cancellation fee
- **No-show**: May be charged full appointment fee
- **Emergency**: Call pharmacy to discuss options

### Confirming Appointments

#### Why Confirmation Matters
- Ensures your appointment is still needed
- Helps pharmacy manage their schedule
- Reduces wait times for all patients
- Shows respect for healthcare providers' time

#### How to Confirm

**Method 1: Email Link**
1. **Check your email** 24-48 hours before appointment
2. **Click "Confirm Appointment"** in the email
3. **Confirmation page** opens automatically
4. **Done!** No further action needed

**Method 2: Text Message**
1. **Reply "YES"** to confirmation text message
2. **Receive confirmation** that appointment is confirmed

**Method 3: Portal Login**
1. **Log into your portal**
2. **Go to "My Appointments"**
3. **Click "Confirm"** next to upcoming appointment

**Method 4: Phone Call**
1. **Call the pharmacy** during business hours
2. **Provide your name** and appointment details
3. **Confirm with staff** member

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Booking and Scheduling

**Q: How far in advance can I book an appointment?**
A: You can typically book appointments up to 3 months in advance. For routine care, we recommend booking 2-4 weeks ahead.

**Q: Can I request a specific pharmacist?**
A: Yes, you can request a specific pharmacist when booking. However, availability may be limited, and you might need to be flexible with dates and times.

**Q: What if I need an urgent appointment?**
A: For urgent needs, call the pharmacy directly. They may have same-day availability or can provide guidance on whether you need immediate medical attention.

**Q: Can I book appointments for family members?**
A: You can book appointments for minor children or adults for whom you have legal healthcare authority. Each person needs their own portal account for privacy and security.

### Technical Issues

**Q: I'm having trouble logging in. What should I do?**
A: First, check that you're using the correct email and password. If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" link. If problems persist, contact support.

**Q: The website isn't working properly. What's wrong?**
A: Try refreshing the page, clearing your browser cache, or using a different browser. Check our status page at status.pharmacycopilot.com for any known issues.

**Q: I'm not receiving email or text reminders.**
A: Check your spam/junk folder for emails. For text messages, ensure your phone number is correct and you haven't blocked messages from our system.

### Appointments and Services

**Q: What should I do if I'm running late?**
A: Call the pharmacy as soon as possible. They may be able to accommodate a short delay or reschedule you for later in the day.

**Q: Can I bring someone with me to my appointment?**
A: Yes, you're welcome to bring a family member or friend for support. Please mention this when booking if they'll need a chair or if there are space considerations.

**Q: Do you accept my insurance?**
A: Insurance acceptance varies by service. Check with your insurance provider or call the pharmacy to verify coverage for specific services.

**Q: What if I need to cancel at the last minute due to illness?**
A: Call the pharmacy immediately. They understand that illness happens and will work with you to reschedule without penalty.

### Privacy and Security

**Q: Is my information secure?**
A: Yes, we use bank-level encryption and follow all HIPAA privacy requirements to protect your health information.

**Q: Who can see my appointment information?**
A: Only authorized pharmacy staff involved in your care can access your information. We never share your data with third parties without your consent.

**Q: Can I delete my account?**
A: Yes, you can request account deletion by contacting support. Note that some information may be retained for legal and medical record requirements.

### Billing and Payment

**Q: How do I pay for my appointment?**
A: Payment methods vary by service. Some are covered by insurance, others may require payment at the time of service. Payment options typically include cash, card, or insurance billing.

**Q: Will I receive a bill?**
A: If your service isn't fully covered by insurance, you'll receive a bill. You can also view billing information in your portal under "Billing & Insurance."

**Q: Can I get a receipt for my appointment?**
A: Yes, receipts are available in your portal after your appointment, or you can request a printed receipt during your visit.
